Questions to help with goal ideas

What is something I struggle with?
What is a talent I like to work on?
What is a talent I have always wanted to try?
What activity could help me grow more physically?
What activity could help me grow more spiritually?
What activity could help me grow more intelectually?
What activity could help me grow more socially?
What is something I could achieve in the next week?
What do I want to accomplish this year?
Where do I want to be in 5 (or 10) years?
What kind of habit would I like to change?
What have I wanted to do but am afraid to try?
If I found out that I only had 6 months to live, what would I do differently?
What are my values and priorities?
What do I most enjoy doing?
